Key Specifications

Planned with practical industrial specifications to support warehousing, assembly, showroom use and day-to-day operational efficiency.

Ceiling Height Approx. 20 ft ground floor height with strong double-volume potential for racking, showroom presentation or added flexibility.
Floor Loading Up to 10 kN/m² on ground floor and 3 kN/m² on mezzanine / upper areas for operational practicality.
Power Supply 100 Amp 3-phase power supply, with selected units reportedly offering higher capacity based on requirements.
Roller Shutter Large motorized loading shutter designed for efficient goods movement and industrial access.
Ramp System Direct ramp-up concept with gentle gradient for smoother up/down access and better loading convenience.
Parking / Bays Minimum 3 dedicated parking or loading bays per unit for staff, visitors and operational use.
Lifts Multiple passenger, cargo, bomba and service lifts planned to support vertical circulation across blocks.
Title Freehold strata-titled industrial units in a large-scale multi-level development.
